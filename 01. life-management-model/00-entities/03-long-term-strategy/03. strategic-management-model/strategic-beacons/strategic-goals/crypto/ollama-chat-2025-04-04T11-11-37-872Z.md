6. **Withdraw Fiat**: After successfully exchanging your cryptocurrency for fiat, you'll need to withdraw the funds to your linked bank account or debit card. Go to the 'Withdraw' section, select your preferred payment method, enter the amount, and follow the instructions to complete the process.
